Adventure Rapids Rafting

At Adventure Rapids Rafting, we live for the thrill of the river. Our mission is to provide safe, unforgettable white water experiences that connect people with nature and create memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2009 by river guides with a passion for adventure, Adventure Rapids Rafting started with just two rafts on the spectacular rivers of the Pacific Northwest. Today, we proudly operate multiple locations and have safely guided thousands of adventurers down some of the most exciting rapids in the country.
