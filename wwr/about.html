Henga Liambee Desmond Rafting Company

Our Purpose

At Henga Liambee Desmond Rafting Company, our purpose is to provide unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences that connect people with nature while prioritizing safety and environmental stewardship. Our experienced guides are passionate about sharing the thrill of the river with adventurers of all skill levels, creating memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2010 by Henga Liambee Desmond, our company began with a single raft and a dream to share the beauty of whitewater rafting with the world. Starting on the local rivers of the Pacific Northwest, we quickly gained a reputation for exceptional safety standards and personalized adventure experiences that catered to both beginners and seasoned rafters.

Over the past 16 years, we have grown from a small family operation to one of the region's premier rafting companies, now offering expeditions on over 15 different rivers. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards, and we continue to expand our offerings while maintaining the personal touch and dedication to safety that defined our humble beginnings.
